<html><body><section data-type="chapter" class="hsecchapter" data-hederis-type="hsecchapter" id="setup-a-toc" data-pi-attrs="id: setup-a-toc; data-tags: convert,typeset;" role="doc-chapter" data-tags="convert,typeset" data-author-name=" " data-book-title=" " title="Set up a Table of Contents Manually"><p class="hblkp" data-hederis-type="hblkp" id="pqz57msoy">You can customize the text that appears in your Table of Contents by setting it up manually in your Word file; once you&#8217;ve got it set up, Hederis will automatically add the final page numbers for you when you build the PDF. To set up a Table of Contents for Hederis, you should use a combination of bookmarks and hyperlinks. Bookmarks and hyperlinks are built-in features of Word, and allow you to create internal links in your document. The destination of the link should have a Bookmark, and the link itself should be inserted as a hyperlink. Here&#8217;s how:</p><section class="hwprsubsection" data-hederis-type="hwprsubsection" id="pYGxlYkgU" data-type="subsection" title="Create Your Table of Contents"><h1 data-hederis-type="hblktitle" class="hblktitle" id="palrqUFRv">Create Your Table of Contents</h1><p class="hblkp" data-hederis-type="hblkp" id="phkczMtdG">The first step is to create the text that will be included in your Table of Contents.</p><p class="hblkp" data-hederis-type="hblkp" id="pIChd6Hye">You do this the same way you&#8217;d insert any paragraph: simply place your cursor in the document, and start typing. For Hederis, there are some rules about how your Table of Contents should be styled:</p><img data-hederis-type="hblkimg" class="hblkimg" id="pGZb0jXJT" src="toc0_1.png" data-img-src="toc0_1.png"/><p class="hblkp" data-hederis-type="hblkp" id="pitCqb10R">It should be wrapped in a Num List wrapper (see the section on Wrappers).</p><p class="hblkp" data-hederis-type="hblkp" id="pW4VqCC3D">Each TOC item should be styled with one of the 4 following styles:</p><ul class="hwprbulletlist" data-hederis-type="hwprbulletlist" id="pPhZWSNsj"><li class="hblkuli" data-hederis-type="hblkuli" id="lifBHZN8ZY"><p class="hblkuli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="pqDneNZfc">HED TOC entry - frontmatter</p></li><li class="hblkuli" data-hederis-type="hblkuli" id="liY4Nx8XAR"><p class="hblkuli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="p4H2w7x8O">HED TOC entry - part</p></li><li class="hblkuli" data-hederis-type="hblkuli" id="liLzKltxTM"><p class="hblkuli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="piDQMyYFX">HED TOC entry - chapter</p></li><li class="hblkuli" data-hederis-type="hblkuli" id="licqj9OawT"><p class="hblkuli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="py1wEYzjd">HED TOC entry - backmatter</p></li></ul></section><section class="hwprsubsection" data-hederis-type="hwprsubsection" id="pVwORLMEF" data-type="subsection" title="Insert Bookmarks"><h1 data-hederis-type="hblktitle" class="hblktitle" id="pQDcNNSkj">Insert Bookmarks</h1><p class="hblkp" data-hederis-type="hblkp" id="pkp3vLBpd">You also need to insert a Bookmark before every heading that you want to include in your Table of Contents. The general steps will be as follows:</p><ol class="hwprnumlist" data-hederis-type="hwprnumlist" id="piI5N2v38"><li class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblkoli" id="liBk6QYhGT"><p class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="pXe9BFwuq">Scroll through your document to find the heading that you want to link to.</p></li><li class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblkoli" id="liq1k2i2KK"><p class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="pZzlLsGcL">Click before the first character of the heading text.</p><img data-hederis-type="hblkimg" class="hblkimg" id="puqRPKHUD" src="toc1_1.png" data-img-src="toc1_1.png"/></li><li class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblkoli" id="liGESsr4R4"><p class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="p5ZcbF16g">Go to Insert &gt; Bookmark&#8230;</p><img data-hederis-type="hblkimg" class="hblkimg" id="pxeN48Fmn" src="toc1_2.png" data-img-src="toc1_2.png"/></li><li class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblkoli" id="liZP14NRFa"><p class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="pqC7pJMct">In the &#8220;Bookmark name&#8221; field, type a name for your Bookmark, and then click &#8220;Add&#8221;.</p></li></ol><p class="hblkp" data-hederis-type="hblkp" id="pichnPTot">You won&#8217;t see anything happen, but when you go to insert your hyperlink, you&#8217;ll see the new Bookmark in your list of Bookmarks.</p><img data-hederis-type="hblkimg" class="hblkimg" id="pdnwf2mXT" src="toc1_3.png" data-img-src="toc1_3.png"/></section><section class="hwprsubsection" data-hederis-type="hwprsubsection" id="pS63MoGEB" data-type="subsection" title="Insert Hyperlinks"><h1 data-hederis-type="hblktitle" class="hblktitle" id="pZwk13cFo">Insert Hyperlinks</h1><p class="hblkp" data-hederis-type="hblkp" id="p0BzUX7eX">Finally, we&#8217;ll create the TOC links. Go back to the Table of Contents text that you added in the first step and do the following:</p><ol class="hwprnumlist" data-hederis-type="hwprnumlist" id="pWh2MLNyv"><li class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblkoli" id="liVYSpap9f"><p class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="pP07hOJBF">Select the whole text of one of your TOC items.</p></li><li class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblkoli" id="li8Rgh6ZZN"><p class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="pAg60DqYX">Go to Insert &gt; Hyperlink&#8230;</p><img data-hederis-type="hblkimg" class="hblkimg" id="p4TTQdgPL" src="hyperlink1.png" data-img-src="hyperlink1.png"/></li><li class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblkoli" id="li5US3gnF0"><p class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="pssLQJIy3">Next to the &#8220;Anchor&#8221; box, click Locate.</p><img data-hederis-type="hblkimg" class="hblkimg" id="phvn20m6v" src="hyperlink2.png" data-img-src="hyperlink2.png"/></li><li class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblkoli" id="liZoHLph2b"><p class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="pXYASNaMk">Expand the &#8220;Bookmarks&#8221; header and choose the Bookmark that you want to link to.</p><img data-hederis-type="hblkimg" class="hblkimg" id="pkSfAJpBI" src="hyperlink4.png" data-img-src="hyperlink4.png"/></li><li class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblkoli" id="lil39R0WoV"><p class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="pc6QjSVGr">Click OK, and then click OK again in the main Hyperlinks dialog box.</p></li></ol><p class="hblkp" data-hederis-type="hblkp" id="peAA0zR1y">Your TOC item will appear blue and underlined, which means that your link was successfully inserted.</p></section></section></body></html>
