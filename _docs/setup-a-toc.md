<html><body><section data-type="chapter" class="hsecchapter" data-hederis-type="hsecchapter" id="setup-a-toc" data-pi-attrs="id: setup-a-toc; data-tags: convert,typeset;" role="doc-chapter" data-tags="convert,typeset" data-author-name=" " data-book-title=" " title="Set up a Table of Contents Manually"><p class="hblkp" data-hederis-type="hblkp" id="p2pWwBsY9">You can customize the text that appears in your Table of Contents by setting it up manually in your Word file; once you&#8217;ve got it set up, Hederis will automatically add the final page numbers for you when you build the PDF. To set up a Table of Contents for Hederis, you should use a combination of bookmarks and hyperlinks. Bookmarks and hyperlinks are built-in features of Word, and allow you to create internal links in your document. The destination of the link should have a Bookmark, and the link itself should be inserted as a hyperlink. Here&#8217;s how:</p><section class="hwprsubsection" data-hederis-type="hwprsubsection" id="pvSnuc2Yo" data-type="subsection" title="Create Your Table of Contents"><h1 data-hederis-type="hblktitle" class="hblktitle" id="pHjnNMqze">Create Your Table of Contents</h1><p class="hblkp" data-hederis-type="hblkp" id="pJ8WlY1Mg">The first step is to create the text that will be included in your Table of Contents.</p><p class="hblkp" data-hederis-type="hblkp" id="pQjjdXBFl">You do this the same way you&#8217;d insert any paragraph: simply place your cursor in the document, and start typing. For Hederis, there are some rules about how your Table of Contents should be styled:</p><img data-hederis-type="hblkimg" class="hblkimg" id="pU5KDIWBi" src="/images/toc0_1.png" data-img-src="/images/toc0_1.png"/><p class="hblkp" data-hederis-type="hblkp" id="pEhnAHNSo">It should be wrapped in a Num List wrapper (see the section on Wrappers).</p><p class="hblkp" data-hederis-type="hblkp" id="pE15kCAPV">Each TOC item should be styled with one of the 4 following styles:</p><ul class="hwprbulletlist" data-hederis-type="hwprbulletlist" id="pDH3Pq1gc"><li class="hblkuli" data-hederis-type="hblkuli" id="liZNulHZ3l"><p class="hblkuli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="pjKhvn1MO">HED TOC entry - frontmatter</p></li><li class="hblkuli" data-hederis-type="hblkuli" id="licvWkjjZx"><p class="hblkuli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="pZUvbn1Yo">HED TOC entry - part</p></li><li class="hblkuli" data-hederis-type="hblkuli" id="limgt8hYE8"><p class="hblkuli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="p5AJsjtrv">HED TOC entry - chapter</p></li><li class="hblkuli" data-hederis-type="hblkuli" id="liGruqann5"><p class="hblkuli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="p8iGWTM5b">HED TOC entry - backmatter</p></li></ul></section><section class="hwprsubsection" data-hederis-type="hwprsubsection" id="p3ixE96fh" data-type="subsection" title="Insert Bookmarks"><h1 data-hederis-type="hblktitle" class="hblktitle" id="pJqvyYm0C">Insert Bookmarks</h1><p class="hblkp" data-hederis-type="hblkp" id="p7CWh1M2J">You also need to insert a Bookmark before every heading that you want to include in your Table of Contents. The general steps will be as follows:</p><ol class="hwprnumlist" data-hederis-type="hwprnumlist" id="pRuSWQF7U"><li class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblkoli" id="li5yK4qV3d"><p class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="p8QmkZUY7">Scroll through your document to find the heading that you want to link to.</p></li><li class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblkoli" id="liFj4hlD92"><p class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="pG7muCqu3">Click before the first character of the heading text.</p><img data-hederis-type="hblkimg" class="hblkimg" id="pclAwStOL" src="/images/toc1_1.png" data-img-src="/images/toc1_1.png"/></li><li class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblkoli" id="liNdqPpc7J"><p class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="poDiTjfXd">Go to Insert &gt; Bookmark&#8230;</p><img data-hederis-type="hblkimg" class="hblkimg" id="pXDpORJAJ" src="/images/toc1_2.png" data-img-src="/images/toc1_2.png"/></li><li class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblkoli" id="lipwujeyEt"><p class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="p4KKSevMU">In the &#8220;Bookmark name&#8221; field, type a name for your Bookmark, and then click &#8220;Add&#8221;.</p></li></ol><p class="hblkp" data-hederis-type="hblkp" id="pAQZ8Xck8">You won&#8217;t see anything happen, but when you go to insert your hyperlink, you&#8217;ll see the new Bookmark in your list of Bookmarks.</p><img data-hederis-type="hblkimg" class="hblkimg" id="patlZ7N58" src="/images/toc1_3.png" data-img-src="/images/toc1_3.png"/></section><section class="hwprsubsection" data-hederis-type="hwprsubsection" id="pjCS87J7N" data-type="subsection" title="Insert Hyperlinks"><h1 data-hederis-type="hblktitle" class="hblktitle" id="p0on4A7HY">Insert Hyperlinks</h1><p class="hblkp" data-hederis-type="hblkp" id="pcMKxHuwv">Finally, we&#8217;ll create the TOC links. Go back to the Table of Contents text that you added in the first step and do the following:</p><ol class="hwprnumlist" data-hederis-type="hwprnumlist" id="poXdKR48U"><li class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblkoli" id="liWHOg26D4"><p class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="pWO9NFatx">Select the whole text of one of your TOC items.</p></li><li class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblkoli" id="li8FVY4N1c"><p class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="pZRimuUcH">Go to Insert &gt; Hyperlink&#8230;</p><img data-hederis-type="hblkimg" class="hblkimg" id="pCHHQ2reN" src="/images/hyperlink1.png" data-img-src="/images/hyperlink1.png"/></li><li class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblkoli" id="liYRsY9IDA"><p class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="pcgQ4GwN4">Next to the &#8220;Anchor&#8221; box, click Locate.</p><img data-hederis-type="hblkimg" class="hblkimg" id="pZNzMYrvK" src="/images/hyperlink2.png" data-img-src="/images/hyperlink2.png"/></li><li class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblkoli" id="liUdeJQjF5"><p class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="pdIkEdo53">Expand the &#8220;Bookmarks&#8221; header and choose the Bookmark that you want to link to.</p><img data-hederis-type="hblkimg" class="hblkimg" id="pFhSWnkzz" src="/images/hyperlink4.png" data-img-src="/images/hyperlink4.png"/></li><li class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblkoli" id="liuVhhz4WR"><p class="hblkoli" data-hederis-type="hblklip" id="pxhmmgzYG">Click OK, and then click OK again in the main Hyperlinks dialog box.</p></li></ol><p class="hblkp" data-hederis-type="hblkp" id="pP69zgafa">Your TOC item will appear blue and underlined, which means that your link was successfully inserted.</p></section></section></body></html>
