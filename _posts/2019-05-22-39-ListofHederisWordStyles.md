    <section class="hwprsubsection" data-hederis-type="hwprsubsection" id="psaWDxDen" data-type="subsection" title="Paragraphs"><h1 data-hederis-type="hblktitle" class="hblktitle" id="pxSOdfMrW">Paragraphs</h1>
    <dl class="hwprdef-list" data-hederis-type="hwprdef-list" id="p4TiFGzxE"><dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pbxF57HCD">HED Address</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="lihHK5VOeC"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pNWsFhUvY">An address, for example in a letter, on a copyright page, etc.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pWfgL1Xx3">HED Attribution</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liYUaEU8UW"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pGImAblaf">The attribution to a quote.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pQtgpUlHy">HED Attribution (contd)</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="li43e1sQJJ"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="ppgVzpg7t">Continuation paragraphs for a quote attribution. An attribution can have multiple continuation paragraphs.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pDFYWquCV">HED Author name</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liXHbilGdR"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pbHssNHn7">The name of an author. This could appear in a variety of places: on the main titlepage, following a preface title or chapter title, etc.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pzVniFknK">HED Box type</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="licVDvAieC"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pJy7pvAFR">Some books include multiple types of boxes and sidebars, that appear multiple times within a book. For example, a book might have a type of box called &#8220;Quick Tips&#8221;, that contains different tips that correspond to the current book topic. This style would be used to tag that Type heading, and is different from the main box heading.</p><img data-hederis-type="hblkimg" class="hblkimg" id="p0QbDIQii" src="/images/boxtype.png"/>
    </dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pzE6fyOKs">HED Caption</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liycpHKuGd"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pvOmaz5UH">A caption, for example to accompany an image or to follow a table.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pjUGw1Uuv">HED Chap/Sect/Wrapper number</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="lip3eGjmcr"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="ppyx5Hv3u">Number labels for any book section or wrapper, e.g., &#8220;Chapter 1&#8221;, or &#8220;Example 12&#8221;.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pUp4fuDfw">HED Chapter/Section title</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liyw5TKvOf"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pKIqxarqf">Titles of main book sections, like chapters, prefaces, appendixes, parts, and so on.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="p3Udagubw">HED Closing</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liWFkz9fYb"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pKUYSa8gd">The closing line of a letter, e.g., &#8220;Sincerely,&#8221;.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="psusSsZjk">HED Code paragraph</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liXaRNko4V"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="p0R8bDcAy">A block of computer code.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pGNQn2fXh">HED Contributor</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="linFgzHxl7"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pDR6YQ8gk">A book or section contributor, other than the author. For example, the name of an illustrator or editor that might appear on the titlepage or at the beginning of a chapter.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pCZOF2Igp">HED Dateline</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liRkQPZdPn"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pCPX7uRgF">A dateline.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="p0xO4J2po">HED Definition definition</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="lixkcxvdmQ"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pKQtVwq2x">The definition paragraph in a set of term/definition pairs. This paragraph is an example of the definition paragraph that corresponds to the term above.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pkw8UAReJ">HED Definition definition (contd)</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="lig7gftOsL"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pwwrAz1fy">A continuation paragraph for a multi-paragraph definition, as defined above.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pK6IVYhpz">HED Definition term</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="lig0eQIGxZ"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pLKqPFYb0">A term to be defined, often followed by a <em>HED Definition </em><em>definition</em> paragraph.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pumO84zXH">HED Dialogue paragraph</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liEyquGzh3"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pU4UdJ4st">Dialogue.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pnDofwXzV">HED Endnote paragraph</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="li0fqAjSYw"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pCYcUAg9d">A paragraph in an endnote.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pfzOvx8jK">HED Footnote paragraph</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="lit6P9z4Bp"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pb5PyrJFj">A paragraph in a footnote.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="p35kYU6S1">HED Generic heading</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liomkh9JJh"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pplF4UBL4">A generic heading, often used in the middle of boxes or sidebars when you want to introduce a block of text without creating a formal sub-section. Also often used as a heading preceding a letter body, e.g., &#8220;CONFIDENTIAL&#8221;.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pk8dYo3Ta">HED Generic subheading</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liiXb9pJsY"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pkrNyYn1o">Similar to the generic heading, a second level of heading that can be used when you want to create a logical grouping of text without creating a formal sub-section.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pNEcLW5G3">HED Image holder</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="li2z89fZQa"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pm5beZ2F9">A placeholder for an image. See &#8220;<a href="{% post_url 2019-05-22-05-Addanimage %}"><span class="Hyperlink">Add an image</span></a>&#8221; for more.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="p9j6a77Er">HED Image source</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liK5oYrxLH"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pGMd4lxVl">The source of an image, e.g. &#8220;Courtesy of Getty Images&#8221;.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pHhlu9zao">HED Imprint name</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liiPRvdwrr"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="p8EessSeN">The name of your imprint, often included on a titlepage.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pssbZghnE">HED Line space</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liOOeVpO4v"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pKA1wPG4h">A line space, often used to create a logical break in time or context.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="p36RyqSd2">HED List item (contd)</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liie0DpzWI"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pjyD7wCek">A second paragraph in a multi-paragraph list item (can be used for both bulleted and numbered list items).</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pXkXlR20W">HED List item &#8211; Bulleted</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liETBgz7aI"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pevFBuntz">A bulleted or unordered list item.     </dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pYUyVVW3Y">HED List item &#8211; Numbered</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liGvvqWRrS"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pQt3iCfAQ">A numbered or ordered list item. For multi-level lists, use the <em>HED BOX </em><em>Num</em><em> List</em> wrapper, nesting multiple levels of list wrappers within it, like this:</p><img data-hederis-type="hblkimg" class="hblkimg" id="plzcV95Cl" src="/images/nestnumlist.png"/>
    </dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="p1IzdPquA">HED Location</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liJmHV6Hdv"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pvRpp9bzm">A location, for example the location of your office as it appears on the book titlepage, or the location in which a chapter is set.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pss7wHuxr">HED Mark for Deletion</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="litanxaHZ5"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="p8bMIzapy">A paragraph that should be deleted in the final output.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="p3fTP34QZ">HED Ornamental space</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="li3SgqvzTQ"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pd2Zczh66">An ornamental break in the text, often used to create a logical break in time or context and containing a visual ornament.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pQTSgKXP1">HED Plain text paragraph</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="lisTedWn7n"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pfmKoopjt">This versatile style is used for plain text, for example in the main flow of a chapter, or a paragraph of text in an extract, or the body text of a letter. See &#8220;<a href="{% post_url 2019-05-22-13-ParagraphsWrappersandSections %}"><span class="Hyperlink">Paragraphs, Wrappers, and Sections</span></a>&#8221; for more about this.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pUwmcMeWZ">HED Plain text paragraph (contd)</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liZ49P1xNb"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pp43yqEIH">If a paragraph is broken up by an image or other content, use this style for the continuing text of the paragraph.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pMEfaXteS">HED Postscript</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="lioPFqtFCx"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pA7dsxXBu">A postscript, as in a letter or foreword, e.g., &#8220;P.S., don&#8217;t forget to buy milk!&#8221;.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="p0CEHdvR5">HED Processing instruction</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liNftbztci"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="paLNtVTF6">This special Hederis style is used to add extra instructions about how to transform the paragraphs and sections of your manuscript. See &#8220;<a href="{% post_url 2019-05-22-24-Addspeciallayoutinstructions %}"><span class="Hyperlink">Add special layout instructions</span></a>&#8221; to learn all about processing instructions.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pz3R7Zup5">HED Publisher name</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liO8MhErOG"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pSsZOgKzk">The name of your publisher, for example appearing on a titlepage.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pPnaoYGGL">HED Quote paragraph</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="lijlLPdKAf"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pcOgzaZ7t">A quote, that you don&#8217;t necessarily want to appear inside an extract.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pPIlmg81A">HED Salutation</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="li5r5Zr9ja"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pe5JIhGPg">A salutation, for example in a letter or foreword, e.g. &#8220;Dear reader,&#8221;.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pQJMtDIk4">HED Signature</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liBsUygQ2S"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pQrLmD6CB">The signature of the author of a block of text, for example in a letter or foreword.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="p8ylSfu1U">HED Speaker name</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="li2LMmJ0x9"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pBW96MnF8">A speaker in a block of dialogue.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pkWsmWmhZ">HED Subtitle</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="li475x9CqA"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="p7eRjGREM">Subtitles for any type of section or wrapper. For example, could be used both for a chapter subtitle, and for a subtitle in a sidebar.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pvFktTFmD">HED TOC entry &#8211; backmatter</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liNkXLx9e0"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="p6RRLpX7P">A table of contents entry for a backmatter section.</p></dd>

    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liAWsTU1Sb"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pKceLCq4o">A table of contents entry for a chapter.</p></dd>

    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liBnyjDtIe"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pOtXmo2kG">A table of contents entry for a frontmatter section.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pr7Dt9jlG">HED TOC entry &#8211; part</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liUg9LRTcZ"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="psOR0SBBL">A table of contents entry for a part.</p></dd>

    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liZ10ww7aj"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="ppeeLoGwC">A single line of verse.</p></dd>

    <section class="hwprsubsection" data-hederis-type="hwprsubsection" id="pZlbkYM4y" data-type="subsection" title="Inline Styles"><h1 data-hederis-type="hblktitle" class="hblktitle" id="poc9wTYJX">Inline Styles</h1>
    <p class="hblkp" data-hederis-type="hblkp" id="pob7onJIP">Inline styles are styles that you apply just to a few words or characters within a paragraph, without affecting the rest of the text in the paragraph. Hederis supports most of Word&#8217;s built-in character formatting tools (like italics, bold, underline, etc.), so you don&#8217;t need to use inline styles for everything. However, there are a few cases where inline styles come in handy:</p>
    <dl class="hwprdef-list" data-hederis-type="hwprdef-list" id="pdCk5fMJ6"><dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pW78gEqBz">HED SPAN Loosen spacing</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liAq8Pum9h"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pLNXkVwCn">Loosen the character spacing in a span of text in the PDF. See &#8220;<a href="{% post_url 2019-05-22-29-AdjustletterspacingPDF-only %}"><span class="Hyperlink">Adjust letter spacing (PDF-only)</span></a>&#8221; for more.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pRDGmvhtk">HED SPAN Tighten spacing</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liqqfJxHkJ"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pYY5wo1SP">Tighten the character spacing in a span of text in the PDF. See &#8220;<a href="{% post_url 2019-05-22-29-AdjustletterspacingPDF-only %}"><span class="Hyperlink">Adjust letter spacing (PDF-only)</span></a>&#8221; for more.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pQZSfAPdG">HED SPAN Line break after</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="li05dpjfK8"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="prAqGTBk1">Insert a line break after a word or character. See &#8220;<a href="{% post_url 2019-05-22-30-ForcealinebreakPDF-only %}"><span class="Hyperlink">Force a line break (PDF-only)</span></a>&#8221; for more.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pJAslV4ox">HED SPAN Page break after</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="lie3X0CTTn"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pR1kISFeh">Insert a line break after a word or character. See &#8220;<a href="{% post_url 2019-05-22-31-ForceapagebreakPDF-only %}"><span class="Hyperlink">Force a page break (PDF-only)</span></a>&#8221; for more.</p></dd>

    <dl class="hwprdef-list" data-hederis-type="hwprdef-list" id="pm6kGrjlL"><dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pJAtgiFS0">HED BOX Box start, HED BOX Box end</dt>
