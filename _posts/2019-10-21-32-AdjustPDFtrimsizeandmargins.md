    <p class="hblkp" data-hederis-type="hblkp" id="pJVc4nAAg">Go to your Project Dashboard and expand the option labeled &#8220;Configure Export Settings&#8221;. </p>
    <p class="hblkp" data-hederis-type="hblkp" id="pgLkg5CSk">To adjust your PDF trim size, enter your chosen values in the height and width boxes, select a unit of measurement from the dropdown options. Your selection is auto-saved.</p>
    <p class="hblkp" data-hederis-type="hblkp" id="p2fc0PVNP">To adjust PDF margin widths, enter values for each of the margins that you&#8217;d like to adjust.</p>
