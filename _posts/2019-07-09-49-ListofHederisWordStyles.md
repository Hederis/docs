    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="li2P2GRBvQ"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="ptIDTm8mq">A postscript, as in a letter or foreword, e.g., &#8220;P.S., don&#8217;t forget to buy milk!&#8221;.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="piumen2Ah">HED Processing instruction</dt>

    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liq0G3qNig"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="ptAjaVpsh">The name of your publisher, for example appearing on a titlepage.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pCyueNwF8">HED Quote paragraph</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liuQNXP878"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="p0cgpYZdK">A quote, that you don&#8217;t necessarily want to appear inside an extract.</p></dd>

    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liyDu3nxCm"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pABg6wOsC">A salutation, for example in a letter or foreword, e.g. &#8220;Dear reader,&#8221;.</p></dd>
    <dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pgbarYt1e">HED Signature</dt>
    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="liIc63ewK6"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="p74LHU6Ab">The signature of the author of a block of text, for example in a letter or foreword.</p></dd>

    <p class="hblkp" data-hederis-type="hblkp" id="p5msjXenJ">Inline styles are styles that you apply just to a few words or characters within a paragraph, without affecting the rest of the text in the paragraph. Hederis supports most of Word&#8217;s built-in character formatting tools (like italics, bold, underline, etc.), so you don&#8217;t need to use inline styles for everything. However, there are a few cases where inline styles come in handy:</p>

    <dd class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="lirNC725tB"><p class="hblkdefinition" data-hederis-type="hblkdefinition" id="pvPV49cRt">Tighten the character spacing in a span of text in the PDF. See &#8220;<a href="{% post_url 2019-07-09-39-AdjustletterspacingPDF-only %}"><span class="Hyperlink">Adjust letter spacing (PDF-only)</span></a>&#8221; for more.</p></dd>

    <dl class="hwprdef-list" data-hederis-type="hwprdef-list" id="pWeFjltvr"><dt data-hederis-type="hblkdefterm" class="hblkdefterm" id="pJoR8dMqL">HED BOX Box start, HED BOX Box end</dt>
